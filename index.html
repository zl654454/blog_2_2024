AI wins big at the Nobels

Article link here 10th October

The 2024 Nobel Prizes recognized groundbreaking AI contributions, with John Hopfield and Geoffrey Hinton awarded the Physics Prize for their work on artificial neural networks, foundational to modern AI. Demis Hassabis and John Jumper from DeepMind, along with David Baker, received the Chemistry Prize for their development of AlphaFold, which predicts protein structures, revolutionizing biological research. The Physiology or Medicine Prize went to Victor Ambros and Gary Ruvkun for their discovery of micro-RNA, which regulates gene expression. These awards highlight the growing influence of AI in science and its potential to drive future breakthroughs.
